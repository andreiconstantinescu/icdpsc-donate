About Change Life

Change Lifeis an educative-cultural-social and mentorship programme meant for the orphan children and those from the vulnerable environment of Romania.

The goal of the programme Change Life is to support and assist financially the children and teenagers until the day they are able to make a living on their own.

Why is Change Life different?

It is so due to its durability and consistency over a longer period of time: 7 years, 10 years, and 15 years, depending on the age of the children as we encourage them over their childhood, teenage years and adult life to make a living on their own.

Change Life is one of the longest voluntary programs because of its daily engagement in the children's life.

Change Life is constantly helped by a multidisciplinary research team who advocates for the creation and the implementation of educational programmes for the improvement of the life quality of these children.

Who are the mentors?

They are psychologists, economists, doctors, programmers, athletes, directors, lawyers, mathematicians, historians, professors, students and anybody who can make a moral engagement through which to be active in the children's life by giving them parental models.

"Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime".

Support Change Life

Support Change Life for the construction of the educational-cultural and recreational centre for the children.
The building of the educational centre is essential for the children because they need to have access to the complex services that contain: psychological and medical assistance offices, study rooms, a recreational room, a reading room and library, personal development courses for children, computer courses, career guidance, analysis of the adolescents’ educational development (looking for a job, preparing for the interview, getting a job).
Many of these children have intellectual possibilities and the desire to study, but because of their social-familial context they get into critical situations becoming illiterate, thugs or even homeless.

Education is the only chance for these orphan children and those from the vulnerable environments!

Transparenta:

Oferim transparenta programului Change Life pe pagina noastra de facebook si pe website, unde puteti observa activitatile programului; construirea centrelor educationale sau puteti fi alaturi de noi prin implicare directa (voluntariat,mentorat) sau indirecta prin donatii, etc.

Si prin programul TRAVEL DONATEputeti face excursii in tara noastra, pornid de la ideea ca puteti calatori cunoscund o tara cu peisaje superbe din Europa, sigur, asigurandu-va de calitatea serviciilor printr-un ghid care sa va ajute sa petreceti o vacanta de neuitat.(cele mai bune restaurante, cele mai importante locuri de vizitat, informatii despre cultura orasului, cultura tarii etc)

Travel Donate este o vacanta cu un scop nobil,caritabil sustinand copiii sa se bucure de un viitor mai bun.

În prezent I.C.D.P.S.C. prin programul Change Life facilitează cursuri gratuite zilnice de recreere, socializare și dezvoltare armonioasă prin înot. Proiectul se adresează unui număr de 138 de copii aflați în grija centrelor de plasament din București, prin cursuri de inițiere în înot, dezvoltare personală și trecerea de la starea de dependentă la independență. Cursurile de înot se desfășoară în cadrul bazinului Federației Române de Polo, fiind coordonate de personal multidisciplinar calificat (voluntari, psihologi, avocați, antrenori etc). Aceste cursuri au că scop oferirea sprijinului și șansei în descoperirea identității și a carierei profesionale pe bază propriilor resurse.
